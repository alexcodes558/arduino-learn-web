Lesson 3: Ultimate Arduino Lesson

Lesson Overview

In this lesson, you will explore advanced Arduino concepts through multiple sensors and outputs. You will learn how to use:

  • Digital buttons
  • Light sensors
  • Temperature sensors
  • Motion (PIR) sensors
  • Ultrasonic distance sensors
  • Potentiometers (analog inputs with PWM outputs)

Each section contains reading material, interactive demos, diagrams, code examples, and quizzes to reinforce your learning.

Arduino & Breadboard Basics

Arduino UNO has digital pins (D0-D13), analog pins (A0-A5), 5V/3.3V power, and GND pins. Breadboards allow easy prototyping without soldering.

Pin Functions

PinTypeExample
D2Digital InputButton
D3Digital I/O / PWMLED / Motor
D4-D7Digital I/OLEDs, buzzer
A0-A5Analog InputLight, Temp, Potentiometer
5V / 3.3VPowerPower sensors
GNDGroundComplete circuit
Tip: Always connect GND from sensors to Arduino ground to complete the circuit.
